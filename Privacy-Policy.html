Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

BlinkRead ("we," "our," or "us") is a speed reading application developed and operated by Re:sauce. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you use our mobile application. This app displays advertisements through Google AdMob to support the free version.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Files you import: When you use the file picker to import PDF, EPUB, or TXT files, these files are processed locally on your device. We do not upload or store these files on any external server.
  • Reading preferences: Settings you configure (themes, font size, reading speed, etc.) are stored locally on your device using Android DataStore.
  • Bookmarks: Any bookmarks you create are stored locally in the app's database.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you interact with advertisements displayed through Google AdMob, certain information may be collected including:

  • Device identifiers
  • Ad interaction data
  • IP address
  • Device information

This data is processed by Google AdMob in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

3. How We Use Information

All data collected is used solely for providing the app's functionality:

  • Reading progress: To remember where you stopped reading and allow you to continue
  • Bookmarks: To save and display your bookmarks within the app
  • User preferences: To apply your chosen theme, font settings, and reading configuration
  • Statistics: To track your reading statistics (words read, time spent) stored locally
  • Advertising: To display relevant advertisements and support the free version of the app

4. Data Storage & Security

  • Local storage only: All data is stored locally on your device using Android's encrypted storage (DataStore) and local database (Room).
  • No external transmission: We do not transmit any data to external servers.
  • Advertising data: Processed by Google AdMob, not stored locally by BlinkRead.
  • No cloud backup of app data: While Android may back up app data to Google Drive, this is under your personal Google account and is not accessible to us.

5. Permissions We Request

Permission Purpose
Storage Access Used only when you explicitly choose to open a file via Android's built-in file picker

We do not request any other permissions (camera, location, contacts, etc.).

6. Third-Party Services

BlinkRead uses Google AdMob for displaying advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Device identifiers
  • Ad interaction data
  • IP address
  • Device information

For more information about how Google AdMob handles data, see Google's Privacy Policy.

7. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us and we will delete it.

8. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control:

  • You can delete the app to remove all local data
  • You can clear app data in Android Settings → Apps → BlinkRead → Clear Data
  • Uninstalling the app removes all stored data

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you by updating the "Last Updated" date above.
