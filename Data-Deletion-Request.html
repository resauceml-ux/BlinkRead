4. How to Delete Your Data

Method 1: Clear App Data (Preserves App)

Deletes all local data but keeps the app installed:

  1. Open Android Settings
  2. Go to Apps → BlinkRead
  3. Tap Storage (or Storage & cache)
  4. Tap Clear Data (or Clear storage)
  5. Confirm the action

This removes all reading progress, bookmarks, preferences, statistics, achievements, challenge progress, clipboard saves, scanned documents, and session history. The app remains installed with default settings.

Method 2: Uninstall the App

Permanently removes all local data:

  1. Open Android Settings
  2. Go to Apps → BlinkRead
  3. Tap Uninstall
  4. Confirm the action

This removes all local data permanently, including any screen recordings saved to app storage.

Method 3: Delete AdMob / Firebase Data from Google

Data collected by Google AdMob, Firebase Analytics, and Firebase Crashlytics is stored on Google's servers. To delete this data:

  1. Visit Google Account Data & Privacy
  2. Sign in with the Google account used on your device
  3. Under Data from apps and services you use, find and manage data associated with BlinkRead
  4. Go to Ad settings → toggle off Ad Personalization
  5. Go to Activity controls → Web & App Activity → Delete activity by → select the relevant time range
  6. For Firebase data: visit Google Firebase support for data deletion instructions
Note: AdMob and Firebase data is managed entirely by Google. BlinkRead does not have access to or control over this data. You must contact Google directly to delete it. Alternatively, email us and we will forward your request to Google on your behalf.

Method 4: Email Deletion Request

For a formal data deletion request, or if you need assistance:

  1. Send an email to Resauce.ml@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject line: "BlinkRead Data Deletion Request"
  3. Include your device ID or Google account email (optional, helps us verify your request)
  4. We will respond within 7 business days

Since all app data is stored locally on your device, we will guide you through Methods 1-3. For AdMob/Firebase data, we will forward your request to Google.
