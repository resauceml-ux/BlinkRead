Day 1 Pahinga ka Muna
Hindi lahat ng pagod, tulog lang ang solusyon .

May mga araw na kahit anong tulog ang gawin mo,
pagod pa rin . You sleep early, you eat well, you barely
move and yet, pagbukas ng mata mo, mabigat pa rin
yung dibdib. Hindi siya yung tipong sore muscles from
walking too much, or headache from too much screen
time. It s something else. Something deeper. Yung pagod
na hindi mo basta malalagay sa isang category hindi lang
physical, hindi lang mental, pero halo halo na.

Minsan, bigla na lang siya darating sa gitna ng
simpleng gawain. Naghuhugas ka lang ng pinggan, tapos
may wave of exhaustion na parang sinabayan ng
lungkot. Or habang naka upo ka sa kama, hawak yung
phone, tapos napansin mo , wala ka talagang gana mag
reply kahit sa pinakamalapit mong kaibigan . It s not
because you don t care. It s because you don t have the
energy to be a person right now.

And let s be real , hindi lahat ng pagod ay galing sa
trabaho o school. Sometimes it s from carrying invisible
weights. The expectation to have it together all the time.
The need to keep smiling so people don t ask questions
you re not ready to answer. Yung pressure na mag
perform, mag excel, magpakita ng progress, kahit wala
ka nang maibigay.
It s the tiredness that comes from feeling too much.
From overthinking every conversation, every choice,
every what if . Yung paulit ulit mong iniisip kung mali
ba yung sinabi mo kahapon. Yung replay ng mga
scenarios sa utak mo habang naliligo ka. Yung walang
katapusang baka at sana na parang ayaw umalis sa
isip mo kahit anong gawin mo.

Kaya kahit magpahinga ka physically , kahit mahiga
ka buong hapon , hindi pa rin siya nawawala. Kasi hindi
katawan mo ang kailangan ng pahinga, kundi ikaw
mismo. Your whole being. Your heart that s been too
busy pretending it s fine. Your mind that s been on
overdrive since who knows when.

We live in a world na parang productivity ang
sukatan ng worth. Kapag wala kang ginawa, parang wala
ka ring halaga. Kapag hindi mo natapos lahat sa to do
list mo, automatic na may guilt. Kaya kahit pagod na
pagod ka na, you push yourself to keep going , just so you
won t feel lazy. Pero alam mo, hindi tamad ang tawag
doon. Being a human ang tawag doon.

Hindi mo kailangan hintayin na maging ubos na ubos
ka bago ka magpahinga. You deserve to pause kahit wala
ka pang natatapos. You deserve to step back kahit
walang valid excuse. Minsan, the reason is as simple as
this, pagod ka na. And that s enough reason.
Na try mo na ba yung tipong mag isa ka sa kwarto
mo, lights off, walang ingay, tapos doon mo lang
mararamdaman yung bigat Kasi habang may ginagawa
ka, busy ka, hindi mo siya ramdam masyado. Pero the
moment you stop, the exhaustion hits you like a wave.
Hindi siya laging malakas. Minsan nga, it s quiet. But it s
heavy.

It s the kind of tiredness na hindi mo ma share sa
lahat. Kapag sinabi mong Pagod ako , sasabihin nila ,
Matulog ka lang . Pero alam mo na hindi lang yun.

Kaya minsan, you stop explaining. You stop trying to
make them understand. You just keep it inside , which,
ironically, makes you even more tired.

Minsan, nakakapagod din yung pagiging okay sa
harap ng ibang tao. Yung kailangan mong ngumiti sa
meeting kahit gusto mo na lang matulog. Yung kailangan
mong mag Haha sa GC kahit wala ka sa mood. Yung
kailangan mong magpakita ng lakas sa pamilya kahit
gusto mo na lang umiyak sa kusina habang naghuhugas
ng tasa.

And maybe, the reason you re this tired is because
you ve been carrying too much , things you didn t even
realize were heavy. Mga bagay na hindi mo naman
obligasyon pero ikaw pa rin ang gumagawa. Mga taong
hindi mo naman responsibilidad pero ikaw pa rin ang
nag aalaga. Problems that aren t yours to fix, pero
somehow, napasayo.

Kaya ngayong araw, I want you to give yourself
permission to slow down. Wag mong i measure ang
value mo sa dami ng na accomplish mo. Hindi mo
kailangang maging productive para maging worthy.
Hindi mo kailangang magpakita ng output para maging
mahalaga.

If today, all you can do is breathe, drink water, and stay
alive that s still enough. That s still something.

Pahinga is not a luxury , it s a necessity. And hindi
lang pahinga sa katawan , pahinga sa isip, sa puso, sa
lahat. Yung pahinga na hindi lang para gumising ka ulit
na may energy, pero para bumalik yung sense of self mo.

Para maalala mo na may buhay ka outside of work,
outside of deadlines, outside of proving yourself.

So, go ahead. Put your phone on silent. Wag ka
munang mag reply sa lahat. Don t open your email.
Don t force yourself to be on . Uminom ka ng kape o
tsaa slowly. Listen to a song na walang lyrics, just
melody. O tumingin ka lang sa bintana, count the clouds,
feel the air. Minsan, that s the medicine.

Hindi mo kailangang hintayin na maging burnout
para magpahinga. Rest now, habang kaya pa. Rest now,
para hindi ka tuluyang maputol sa gitna. Rest now, kasi
you re human and that s all the reason you need.

Kung ngayon, pakiramdam mo kulang ka , hindi totoo
yon. Hindi ka kulang. Pagod ka lang. And you are
allowed to rest. You are allowed to exist without
performing. You are allowed to be still, to be quiet, to
just be.

And never mong kalimutan , you are not a machine. You
don t need to earn your right to pause. So, Pahinga ka
muna.














Day 2 Hindi Ka Late, May
Sarili Kang Timing
Just because you re not where others are doesn t mean you re
behind.

Time flies, no Parang kailan lang, ang gaan gaan pa
ng lahat. Dati, ang problema mo lang ay kung saan kayo
magla lunch ng barkada mo. Dati, naglalaro ka lang sa
labas, umiiyak ka lang kapag nasugatan ka , ngayon iba
na yung iniiyakan mo , something much deeper na , Bigla
na lang tayong tumanda. Bigla na lang lumayo ang lahat.
Bigla na lang ang dami nang pressure sa paligid mo.
Minsan, parang hindi mo na alam kung paano ka
napunta rito.

Tapos, kapag tumingin ka sa paligid mo, or worse, sa
social media, parang lahat may narating na. Yung iba
may stable na trabaho, may sariling kotse, may business,
may love life, may engagement, may anak, may bahay.

Yung iba may title na sa pangalan nila, o nasa dream
job na nila. Tapos ikaw Parang wala namang malaking
nangyari. Parang ikaw na lang yung naiwan.

Same room.
Same routine.
Same you.

Nakakapagod and nakaka dismaya minsan . Kasi
kahit anong pilit mong gumalaw, kahit anong gawin mo,
feeling mo hindi ka umaabot. Wala kang napo post.
Wala kan g achievement unlocked . Wala kang glow
up. Tapos makikita mo pa sa feed mo, yung mga kaklase
mo noon, nagpa publish na ng libro, or may brand na.
Yung iba nagta travel, nagpapakasal, may caption pang
timing is everything .

Tapos ikaw nakatingin lang sa kisame, nagtatanong
sa sarili, Late na ba talaga ako

But Listen, hindi ka late. May sarili kang timing.
Hindi dahil nauna sila, e ibig sabihin naiiwan ka na.

Iba ang landas nila, iba ang sa yo. Hindi naman to
karera. Hindi to contest. Hindi rin to scripted. Walang
fixed deadline ang tagumpay. Walang tamang edad para
maabot ang mga pangarap. Hindi dahil 21 kana, dapat
graduate ka na ng college, Hindi dahil 25 ka na, dapat
may bahay ka na. Hindi dahil 30 ka na dapat may asawa
ka na. Hindi dahil hindi mo pa nararating ang gusto mo,
ibig sabihin hindi ka na makakarating.

And hey, okay lang na ma late. Huwag lang sa trabaho
kasi may kaltas sa sahod, di ba Pero seryoso, okay lang
mapagod. Okay lang mabagal. Okay lang magpahinga.
Okay lang na wala ka pang big win ngayong taon.
Because in the end of the day, darating ka rin sa
pupuntahan mo.

Ang mahalaga huwag kang hihinto. Pwedeng tumigil
sandali. Pwedeng huminga. Pwedeng mag ayos ng sarili.
Pero huwag kang susuko.

Wag mong sabihing wala kang ginagawa, kasi alam
mo sa sarili mong may effort ka. Kahit maliit. Kahit
simple. Kahit hindi napapansin ng iba.

May mga pangarap talaga na matagal ang luto. Pero
hindi ibig sabihin nun ay walang nangyayari. Ang bawat
araw na pinipili mong bumangon, gumalaw, kumapit,
progress yan. Kahit pa maliit, kahit pa mabagal, kahit
walang audience.

Tingnan mo si Colonel Sanders, oo, yung KFC guy.

He started finding success in his 60s. Pero hindi yun
huli. Hindi yun sayang. Kasi sa oras na marating mo ang
tagumpay mo, lahat ng paghihintay, lahat ng pagod,
lahat ng delay, magiging worth it.

Let others run their own race. Hayaan mong sila
muna ang umarangkada.

Support them. Celebrate with them. But never use their
journey to invalidate your own. Kasi ang kwento mo, iba.
Ang pace mo, sarili mong rhythm.

And trust me, your time will come. Your chapter will
open. Your season will bloom. You are not falling
behind. You are simply taking the longer, quieter road,
and there s no shame in that.

You are not too late. You are not too slow. You are
enough and you are on your way. Keep going,

You ll get there. At darating ka, sa sarili mong oras,
hindi sa dikta ng iba, kundi sa sarili mong panahon.











Day 3 The Last Time You Felt
Like a Beginner
You don t have to be good at something to love it.

When was the last time you let yourself be bad at
something

Like, really bad. Clumsy hands, awkward timing,
trying hard but still mali kind of bad.

Kasi minsan, we forget that not everything we do has
to be perfect. Hindi lahat kailangan Instagram ready.
Hindi lahat kailangan pasok sa standard ng worth
posting.

Think about it. The first time you tried to bake
cookies and nakalimutan mong i preheat yung oven. The
batter sat there, looking sad, habang ikaw nakatayo sa
kusina wondering if this is what failure smells like. The
first time you held a guitar and your fi ngers hurt after
two chords, but you kept strumming kahit tunog lata pa.

The first time you joined a class , maybe art, maybe
dance, maybe public speaking , and felt your whole body
tense kasi you didn t know what you were doing.

Nakakahiya, minsan. Nakakainis. Pero diba may
konting thrill Yung parang kahit hindi ka magaling,
buhay na buhay ka sa moment.

Growing up, sanay tayong may grades, may rankings,
may comparison lagi. If you re not the best, parang
sayang lang. Kaya we start avoiding things na hindi tayo
sigurado.

We stop trying new stuff kasi ayaw natin
maramdaman yung awkwardness ng pagiging baguhan.
Ayaw natin ma judge. Ayaw natin magmukhang tanga.

Pero here s the thing being a beginner is one of the
purest forms of being alive. Kasi sa simula, wala pang
pressure. Wala pang expectation. It s just you, curiosity
mo, at yung willingness mong magkamali.

I remember someone telling me once You don t have
to be good at something to love it. And it stuck. Kasi oo
nga naman why do we think kailangan expert agad para
mag enjoy Bakit parang automatic na if I m not good,
I m not allowed to keep doing it

Pero try mo balikan yung last time na beginner ka.
Maybe nag try ka mag roller skate at halos mabuwal ka
tuwing gagalaw. Maybe nagluto ka ng pasta na hindi mo
alam yung boiling time ng noodles kaya ang tigas . Maybe
you drew something and it looked nothing like the
picture in your head, a little bit disappointing , pero
you re satisfied anyway.

In those moments, you weren t doing it para may
patunayan. You were doing it just to see if you could. And
that s beautiful.

Gen Z culture has this weird pressure to monetize or
master every hobby. Nag try ka mag paint Baka gawing
sideline.

Nag picture ka maganda Baka gawing portfolio.
Nag try ka ng new skill Baka gawing content. Parang
hindi na puwede yung for fun lang.

Pero not everything has to be a side hustle. Not
everything has to be shared or evaluated.

Pwede ka lang mag try. Pwede ka lang mag enjoy.
Pwede mong hayaang pangit muna, or forever pangit,
and still keep doing it kasi it makes you happy.

Maybe the last time you felt like a beginner was years
ago. Maybe ngayon mo lang nare realize na hindi mo na
pinapayagan sarili mong magkamali sa harap ng iba. Or
maybe you ve been stuck in routines for so long,
nakakalimutan mo na yung feeling ng unang be ses.

Pero here s the truth , life will always give you first
times, if you re brave enough to say yes. And every first
time is a chance to remember that you re more than your
skill level.

Think of it this way , nung bata ka, halos lahat first
time. First time kumain ng ice cream, first time mag
bike, first time sumubok ng bagong laro. Wala ka
namang iniisip noon kung magaling ka ba. You just did
it. You laughed when you fell, you got up, you tried again.
Somewhe re along the way, natutunan mong i measure
yung sarili mo by how good you are at something.
Pero maybe it s time to unlearn that.

The beauty of being a beginner is that it s temporary
and timeless at the same time. Temporary kasi oo, you ll
get better eventually.

Timeless kasi kahit mag improve ka, there will always be
new things to start from scratch. You re never too old to
be new at something.

So try it. Magluto ka ng dish na hindi mo alam. Mag
try ka mag take ng film photos kahit blurred lahat.
Sumayaw ka sa TikTok trend kahit hindi mo ma
memorize yung steps. Mag drawing ka ng cartoon na
mukhang stick figure.

Do it not for perfection, but for the joy of it.

One day, you ll look back and remember the wobbly
lines, the burned cookies, the off key singing, and you ll
realize those moments mattered more than the polished
ones.

Kasi sa pagiging beginner, you were honest. Walang
mask. Walang script. Just pure, clumsy, awkward,
beautiful effort. You don t have to be good at something
to love it. And you don t have to prove that love to
anyone.

So go ahead. Try again. Fail again. Laugh again. Be a
beginner again.
















Day 4 May Progress Sa Pag
iyak
Crying isn t weakness it s release. Healing happens here too.

Hala, ayan na naman yung iyakin. Oo, ikaw. Ikaw na
nagbabasa. Napakaiyakin mo, no Kailan ka nga ba
huling umiyak Last week Kahapon Kanina Or baka
habang binabasa mo to, may namumuong luha na sa
gilid ng mata mo.

Alam mo, sa totoo lang, crying has such a bad
reputation. Society makes it seem like iyak equals
mahina. Parang kapag nakita ka ng tao na may luha,
automatic iniisip nila Wala to sa ayos, or Grabe, ang
hina naman nito. Pero sino ba nagsabi na weaknes s ang
luhang yan Kasi ako, I see it differently , crying is not a
crack in your strength. It s a doorway. It s a release. It s
proof na may puso ka pa, that you re still alive enough to
feel.

Pero sige, aminin natin. May mga moments talaga na
naiinis ka na rin sa sarili mo. Ang babaw ko , you think.
Napagalitan ka lang ng konti, iyak. May sinabi lang na
medyo masakit, iyak. May naalala kang memory sa jeep
habang may random song na tumutugtog, iyak. Minsan
nga, hindi mo na alam kung anong trigger. Basta na lang
bumibigat yung dibdib mo, tapos ayan na, automatic
tumutulo na yung luha mo.

And then habang umiiyak ka, may isang maliit pero
malakas na boses sa loob ng utak mo na nagbubulong
like Arte mo. A ng hina mo. Ang drama mo na
naman. Grabe, konting bagay lang. It s that inner
critic minsan galing sa sarili mo, minsan galing sa mga
narinig mo growing up. Sa mundo ngayon, ang daming
nagsasabi na Be strong. Stop crying. Don t be too
emotional. Don t let them see you break.

So, anong ending Pinipigilan mo. Tinitiis mo.
Tinatago mo. You swallow your tears until the lump in
your throat becomes part of you. Pero ang hindi mo
alam, the more mong pinipigil, the more na naiipon.
Hanggang isang araw, hindi na lang basta iyak , kundi
breakdown. Yung tipong wala ka nang masabi, wala ka
nang masimulan, iyak na lang ang lumalabas.

Kasi kahit gaano ka pa ka strong sa paningin ng iba,
tao ka pa rin. At ang tao, umiiyak. Normal lang. Natural
lang. It s not something you need to hide. Pero dahil sa
sobrang dami ng invalidating words na naririnig mo,
napapaisip ka Baka nga mali na umiiyak ako. Baka
wala nang sense. Baka wala nang kwenta kahit ilang
luha pa to.

Pero gusto kong sabihin sa yo ngayon may progress
sa pag iyak. Hindi man siya loud. Hindi man siya
obvious agad. Pero healing happens in every tear. Every
drop carries something heavy away from your chest.

Yung pag iyak mo Hindi siya basura. Hindi siya
kababawan . It s your body releasing what your words
can t say. It s your soul trying to speak when everything
else feels too heavy.

Crying isn t the opposite of strength. In fact, it s part
of it.

Imagine mo, sa dami ng iniisip mo, sa dami ng
dinadala mo araw araw work deadlines, family
expectations, pressure to be okay, plus your own
heartbreaks and disappointments tapos sasabihin mo
pa sa sarili mo na wala kang karapatang umiyak

Hindi mo kailangang i justify yung luha mo para
masabing valid siya. You cry because nasasaktan ka.
Because pagod ka. Because may bigat sa loob na hindi
mo kayang bitbitin nang mag isa. At minsan, umiiyak ka
kasi gusto mo lang ng release, ng pahinga.

Sabi nila, Tears won t solve anything . Pero totoo
ba yon Minsan, crying is the first step to solving
something.

Hindi siya ang buong sagot, pero siya ang simula.
Pagkatapos mong umiyak, doon mo mararamdaman na
Okay, may kailangan akong ayusin . or Okay, buhay
pa pala ako deep inside .

Kasi think about it kung hindi ka na umiiyak, baka
naging numb ka na. Baka tinanggap mo na lang lahat
kahit masakit na. Baka pinili mo na lang huwag
maramdaman, kahit unti unti ka nang nababawasan.
And numbness might protect you for a while, pero it also
robs you of your humanity.

Minsan, mas okay pa yung may luha kaysa
magpanggap na okay ka. At least kapag umiiyak ka,
you re being honest with yourself, and maybe with
someone you trust. Hindi mo tinatakpan ng forced smile
or Haha ok lang sa chat. Hindi mo ini edit yung sarili
mo para magmukhang strong palagi.

So kung ngayon, umiiyak ka Please, wag mo pigilan.
Wag mong i apologize. Hindi mo kailangang i explain.
Hindi mo kailangang i compare yung reason mo sa
reason ng iba. Your pain is real. Your tears are valid.

At kapag naiyak mo na lahat, kapag naibuhos mo
kahit kaunti lang, mapapansin mo , kahit papaano,
gumaan. Hindi agad solution, pero simula. And
beginnings matter. Minsan, kailangan mo lang maglabas
para magkaroon ng space sa loob mo for hope, for
clarity, for courage.

So next time na may tumulo na naman na luha, wag
mong tanungin ang sarili mo kung mahina ka. Kasi ang
totoo, pinapalaya mo lang ang bigat na matagal mo nang
kinikimkim.

And if no one else has told you this , I m proud of you.
Hindi dahil umiiyak ka lang , kundi dahil pinili mong
maramdaman. Pinili mong hindi magpanggap. Pinili
mong maging totoo.

At sa mundong mahilig mag invalidate ng emotions , yan
ang tinatawag na tapang.

Now you zoom in sa sarili mong reaction. Should you

Before you know it, two hours have passed , wala ka

Gen Z is especially good at this maybe kasi we grew

while life outside keeps moving.

And let s be gentle here , kasi overthinking doesn t

brain is trying to make sense of the world, kahit messy
siya. It means the moment mattered enough to you to
hold onto.

Maybe the scene you keep replaying is a fight with a

just mad Maybe it s a romantic moment that felt like it

almost touch of their hand on yours. You keep thinking
ako lang ba to Or was it something

Maybe it s a failure you can t let go of. The way you
stumbled on your words during a presentation. The
awkward silence after your joke didn t land , the
nakakahita moments.

You keep asking Do they still remember Or ako na
lang ang naiwan dito

And here s the tricky part , The more you replay it, the
more details you notice , but also, the more details you
invent .

Your brain fills in blanks that might not even be real.
Until the original memory blurs with the version you ve
edited and re edited a hundred times.

Sometimes, overthinking isn t just in your head , it s

re reading receipts. You check the exact timestamp they
left you on seen. You replay that voice note, trying to

you ve squeezed it for meaning and all that s left is static.

And yes, I know it s not as simple as just stop
thinking about it. If it were, you wouldn t be here.

But maybe, the next time the clip starts rolling again,

You are not defined by a single expression, a single

Sometimes, people just say things. Sometimes

You can choose to keep the parts that make you smile.

And d arating yung araw na maalala mo pa rin siya ,

Kasi sa dami ng moments worth replaying, hindi lahat

Day 28 Pagod
It s not just tired . It s everything, all at once.

May mga araw na hindi mo alam kung saan
nanggagaling yung pagod. Hindi siya yung usual na
kasi puyat ka kagabi o kasi ang dami mong ginawa.

It s more like the weight of everything is quietly
sitting on your chest, kahit wala ka namang bitbit
physically.

Parang may invisible tab sa utak mo na may 100 open
pages lahat naglo load, lahat may sound playing
somewhere, and you can t find the pause button.

You wake up, and before mo pa nga ma stretch yung

notifications from three different group chats, dalawang
missed calls, at limang app na may red dot. Hindi ka pa
nga nakakapag toothbrush, pero feeling mo late ka na sa
lahat.

That s the thing in this era, life starts before you re
ready .

Kasi kahit tulog ka, the world is still moving, and
you re scared to miss something.

Kaya minsan, pagod ka kahit wala ka pang ginagawa.
Sa totoo lang, hindi lang ito about work o school.
It s about existing in a world na walang off button.

Your brain is constantly catching things voices, lyrics,
to do lists, reminders and wala kang moment na totally
tahimik.

Silence feels unnatural now, parang may mali kapag
walang tunog. Pero ironically, kahit surrounded ka ng
ingay, you still feel alone.

Parang lahat ng ginagawa mo, kahit may kausap ka,
half present ka lang. Nag hmm ka sa VC para
magmukhang nakikinig, pero sa totoo lang iniisip mo
kung tapos na ba yung file na inupload mo. Nag like ka
ng memes sa group chat, pero wala ka talagang energy
mag reply.

Physical tiredness is one thing. Like when you ve
been up for three nights finishing a project, or yung
tipong pinilit mong mag commute kahit masama
pakiramdam mo.

Pero the kind of tired na nakakabaliw is the mental
one.

Yung buong araw kang nagmu multitask nagta type ka
ng report, may bukas na tab para sa class recording, may
GC ka na nag aalert every 10 seconds, tapos may nag PM
pa about quick question lang .

At the end of the day, hindi mo na maalala kung ano
talaga yung natapos mo. Parang dumaan lang lahat
sa yo, pero wala kang hawak.

And then there s emotional exhaustion. Yung tipong
sa labas, okay ka nagjo joke ka, nag ko comment ng
same sa memes,

nagpapakita ka ng reaction sa stories ng iba. Pero
deep down, parang nauubos ka. Parang may leak yung
energy mo na hindi mo mahanap. Tapos may guilt pa,
kasi iniisip mo, Hindi naman ako dapat mapagod, wala
naman akong masyadong ginawa. Pero yun nga pagod
doesn t always have a visible cause.

The scariest part Pag nasanay ka sa ganitong pagod,
you start to mistake it for normal. You wake up groggy,
scroll for an hour para magising, drink your reheated
coffee twice kasi nalimutan mo na naman inumin.

You sit in front of your laptop, try to start, and
suddenly it s 4 PM and wala ka pang meaningful output.
You tell yourself, Okay, bukas babawi ako, but bukas
just feels like today on repeat.

And let s not pretend na hindi tayo partly guilty. We
choose to check social media kahit late na. We say quick
break sa TikTok pero nag end up ka sa isang rabbit hole
ng random videos.

We open Netflix kahit alam natin na isang episode
lang daw, pero ayun, four hours later, patapos na yung
season.

It s not that we re lazy , we re overstimulated to the point
na hindi na natin alam paano magpahinga nang totoo.

May mga signs na pagod ka na beyond recovery ng
isang tulog lang like pag Nakakalimutan mo kung saan
mo nilagay yung gamit mo, kahit hawak mo lang kanina.
Binasa mo na yung same paragraph apat na beses, pero
blank pa rin yung utak mo. Hindi ka na nagrereply kahit
sa mga taong gusto mong kausapin, kasi wala ka nang
lakas para mag type. And Naiinis ka sa maliliit na bagay
like someone typing too slow, or a lagging call kahit alam
mong hindi naman siya big deal.

And when you reach that point, it s hard to explain to
others. Kasi nga, the world thinks pagod is simple ,
Matulog ka lang. Pero hindi nila alam na minsan, kahit
eight hours ang tulog mo, pag gising mo, parang hindi ka
naman nabawasan ng bigat. Because rest is not just
about closing your eyes. It s about finding quie t and
quiet is rare now.

yourself. Kapag lagi kang drained, you start to believe na
mabagal ka, na hindi ka productive, na wala kang
kwenta.

You start to measure your worth by how much you can
push through exhaustion , as if being tired is proof that
you re trying hard enough.

Pero sa totoo lang, exhaustion is not a badge of
honor.

Hindi siya dapat goal. Sometimes, we don t need more
productivity hacks. We need to stop glorifying the grind.
We need to stop treating rest like a reward you earn after
deserving it. Kasi the truth is, you don t have to earn
your right to breathe.

If you re reading this while your brain feels like a
browser with 37 tabs open , know that it s not just you.
You re not broken for feeling this way. We live in a time
na kahit wala kang ginagawa physically, mentally you re
always on call. It s exhausting to live in a world na every
second, something is asking for your attention.

So if you need to log out without warning, do it. Kung
kailangan mong i mute lahat ng GC for a day, gawin mo.
Kung gusto mong mag stay tahimik sa VC, walang
masama dun. You don t owe the world your constant
presence.

Pagod isn t weakness. It s your body and mind telling
you, Hey, something s not sustainable here. And
maybe that s the real invitation , not just to rest, but to
reconsider how you re living.

Kasi if every day feels like survival mode, baka hindi
mo na nabibigyan ng chance yung sarili mo to actually
live. So take it slow. Drink your coffee while it s still hot.
Watch the sunlight move across your wall.
Let your phone die without rushing to plug it in.
And remember , hindi mo kailangang patunayan sa kahit
sino na kaya mo pang magtiis.

Pagod ka kasi tao ka. At kahit hindi mo sabihin sa iba,
kahit wala silang clue kung gaano kabigat yung dinadala
mo, you have the right to step away, to go offline, to
disappear for a bit.

Not because you gave up. But because you re giving
yourself a chance to come back whole.





Day 29 Di Mo Masabi
Not everything heavy has to be carried out loud .

There are moments when the loudest thing in your

sits rent free in your brain . It s not always about love or
heartbreak. Minsan, it s about all the things you wanted

boundaries you wanted to set , but never did.

Our culture trains us to read the room before we
speak. Mula pagkabata, tinuruan tayo na mag
opo kahit hindi natin gusto yung sinasabi. To smile
politely kahit may kurot sa dibdib. To stay quiet para
hindi magulo . And so, by the time we re adults, we ve
mastered the art of editing ourselves in real time.
You rehearse your truth in your head .
I think I deserve better.
I m overwhelmed.
This isn t working for me.

Pero bago pa man makarating sa bibig, the inner
editor kicks i n, Is it worth the drama Baka hindi nila
maintindihan. Baka ako pa masisi. And so you swallow
it again, filing it away in that mental cabinet labeled
Someday .

And sometimes the people you love are also the
people you fear disappointing, silence becomes your
default language.

Gusto mong sabihin na ayaw mo na sa comparisons.
Yung Bakit di ka tulad ni ganyan na paulit ulit mo
nang naririnig since grade school. You want to answer
back, Because I m me, not them. Pero ang lumalabas

It s not that you don t care about them. In fact, you

You wash the dishes longer than needed, magtatagal sa
kwarto, magpapanggap na busy para lang hindi
magsimula ng conversation na alam mong mauuwi sa
debate.

weight. Hindi mo siya ramdam agad, pero parang bato
sa bulsa unti unti kang hinihila pababa.

Sometimes, the things you can t say are small
aches in your friendships. Maybe you ve noticed na
lumalayo na kayo. Or napapansin mong hindi ka na
isinasama sa plans. You draft a message I feel left out
lately , pero bago mo ma send, nagiging Miss you
guys with a heart emoji.

We think keeping it light will keep the friendship
intact, pero minsan , it just creates more distance. You
laugh at the right parts of the group chat, react to every
meme, and pretend you re fine , even when you re not.
But deep inside, you re wonderin g When did I stop
being part of the real conversations

Friendship breakups rarely have a final scene.
Minsan, it s just a collection of things unsaid.

Love or whatever version of it you re in , has its own
archive of unspoken truths. You might want to say, I
need clarity, or I can t keep doing this if you re
unsure, pero ang lumalabas sa chat ay, Kain tayo or
a random Tik Tok link.

Bakit Kasi you know that once it s said, you can t unsay
it.
Once it s in the air, you either move forward or part
ways. And maybe you re not ready for either.

So you choose limbo, masking discomfort with casual
conversations.

But limbo is exhausting. It s like standing on one foot
for months at some point, you ll have to decide where to
land.

In the workplace or in class, di mo masabi can be about
boundaries.

You want to tell your boss, I m burned out, pero
instead, you type Got it, will do . You want to tell your
professor, This deadline isn t realistic, pero you just
reply with Noted po.

It s survival. The system rewards those who don t
complain. But every time you choose silence over
honesty, your body keeps the score shallow breathing,
tension sa shoulders, headaches na parang lagi kang
may bitbit.

Your body remembers the sentences you never said.
The tight jaw from all the I m fine. The stiff shoulders
from No worries, I ll handle it. The shallow breathing
from I can adjust.
Kapag natigil ka sandali maligo, mag commute,
tumambay mag isa your body sometimes whispers the
truth before your mind does Pagod na ako. or I want
out.

And the scary part The longer you ignore it, the
louder it gets in other ways irritability, random
breakdowns, the sudden urge to disappear for a while.

Silence isn t always fear. Sometimes, it s strategy.
Maybe you re waiting for the right time, the right place,
the right words. Maybe you re protecting peace. Maybe
you ve learned that not every battle needs to be fought
out loud.

And that s valid. But here s the caution don t confuse
choosing peace with accepting harm . Silence is healthy
only when it s voluntary, not when it s forced by fear.

You don t have to unload everything at once. Minsan,
speaking starts small like Can I be honest , I m not
okay today . Or I need space .

They re not essays. They re doorways. And once you
step through, you realize the world didn t collapse
sometimes, it even gets lighter.

If saying it to someone feels too big, start elsewhere.
Record a voice note you ll never send. Write in your
Notes app without fixing the typos. Whisper it to the
mirror. Let the words exist somewhere outside your

Not everyone deserves the full version of your story.
Some people will twist it, minimize it, or weaponize it.
And when you sense that, trust yourself enough to hold
back. Your truth deserves a safe landing.

Pero kapag nahanap mo yung tao na kayang
magbuhat ng kwento mo nang hindi ka tinatamaan ,
keep them. They are rare.

One day, you ll say it. Maybe in a cafe, mid coffee. Maybe
on a random fridays night call. Maybe in a shaky DM.

And when you do, your voice might tremble, pero
you ll notice something , your shoulders drop. Your chest
feels roomier. Your jaw unclenches.

That s the thing about unspoken truths , when they
finally leave, they don t just free the air, they free you.

Hindi mo kailangang sabihin lahat ngayon. But
promise me you won t decide never to say it. Keep the
door on a latch. Make space for the braver version of you
to step forward, kahit pabulong lang. Because not
everything heavy has to be carried out loud but nothing
heavy should be carried alone.

mo, ng narrative mo, ng paraan mo sa pag comfort sa
sarili mo.

At kapag dumating yung point na ikaw na yung
nagbibigay ng ganitong klaseng comfort sa iba , mas lalo
kong masasabi na proud ako sa yo.

Pero kung sakaling bumalik ka , dahil gusto mo, hindi
lang dahil kailangan . nandito pa rin ako.

Hindi ko ija judge kung pang ilang balik mo na to.
Hindi ko bibilangin. Kasi ang dami nang bagay sa
mundo na binibilang , grades, likes, deadlines, utang,
productivity

hindi ko na idadagdag sa listahan na yun.

So no, this is not goodbye. It s more like see you when
I see you. Pwede bukas, pwede next week, pwede next
heartbreak, pwede next big win. Kahit anong context,
welcome ka dito.

Kahit ano pa yung dala mo, hindi ka lalabas na empty
handed. Bibigyan ulit kita ng paalala, ng lakas, and ng
mga salitang pwede mong dalhin kahit saan.

Kung sa tingin mo ngayon wala kang maibigay sa
mundo, okay lang , Hindi mo kailangang mag give right
now.

Take.
Take comfort.
Take rest.
Take the words that make breathing easier.

Kasi balang araw, babalik ka rin para magbigay at kapag
dumating yung araw na yon, mas solid yung ibibigay mo
kasi nag alaga ka muna sa sarili mo.

And maybe before you close this book, gawin mo to ,
Look around you. Kung nasa kwarto ka, notice yung
maliit na bagay na nagpapagaan sa feeling mo kahit
kaunti , ilaw na hindi masyadong maliwanag, kumot na
sakto yung bigat, picture sa dingding, plant na kahit
medyo tuyot na, buhay pa rin.

Kung nasa biyahe ka, tingnan mo yung bintana, yung tao
sa kabilang upuan, yung pulang tricycle na mabilis
dumaan, yung street sign na faded na pero nandyan pa
rin.

Little proofs that not everything has to be perfect to still
be here. At ganyan ka rin.

So yeah the next time you need me , just open to any
page.

I might be here as a reminder, or a friend, or a stranger
who somehow says exactly what you needed to hear . And
kahit hindi ko alam yung eksaktong pinagdadaanan mo
sa oras na yon, I hope you feel the same thing you felt .
the first time you came here ,

That this space is yours. And that you are worth coming
back to.















Hanggang sa muli..
