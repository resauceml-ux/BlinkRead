1. Data Stored on Your Device

BlinkRead stores all user data locally on your device. We do not transmit or store any data on external servers.

Data Type Description Deleted on Uninstall?
Reading Progress Book reading position and progress ✅ Yes
Bookmarks Bookmarks you've created in books ✅ Yes
User Preferences Theme, font size, reading speed settings ✅ Yes
Reading Statistics Words read, reading time, WPM history ✅ Yes
Imported Files Books you've imported (processed temporarily) ✅ Yes

2. Data Collected by Google AdMob

BlinkRead uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Device identifiers
  • Ad interaction data
  • IP address
  • Device information
Important: AdMob data is stored on Google's servers and is NOT deleted when you uninstall BlinkRead. You must request deletion directly from Google.

3. How to Delete Your Data

Method 1: Clear App Data (Preserves App)

  1. Open Android Settings
  2. Go to Apps → BlinkRead
  3. Tap Storage
  4. Tap Clear Data
  5. Confirm the action

This deletes all local data but keeps the app installed.

Method 2: Uninstall the App

  1. Open Android Settings
  2. Go to Apps → BlinkRead
  3. Tap Uninstall
  4. Confirm the action

This removes all local data permanently.

Method 3: Delete AdMob Data from Google

To delete data collected by Google AdMob:

  1. Visit Google Account Data & Privacy
  2. Sign in with your Google account
  3. Go to Ad settings or Privacy & personalization
  4. Request deletion of ad-related data
Note: AdMob data is managed by Google, not BlinkRead. You must contact Google directly to delete this data.
